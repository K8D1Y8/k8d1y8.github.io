Multi-modal Learning for Medical Image Analysis

2021 - MICCAI

Authors

John Doe, Dr. Medical, Prof. Imaging

Abstract

Medical image analysis often requires integrating information from multiple modalities to improve diagnostic accuracy. In this paper, we present a novel framework for multi-modal learning that effectively combines features from different imaging modalities (MRI, CT, PET) for improved disease classification and segmentation. Our approach introduces a cross-modal attention mechanism that dynamically weights the contribution of each modality. Evaluation on three public datasets shows significant improvements over single-modality methods and existing fusion approaches.

Keywords

Medical Imaging, Multi-modal Learning, Image Segmentation, Deep Learning
